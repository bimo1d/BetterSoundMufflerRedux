# Changelog

## 0.1b

- Rebuilt for KSP2 Redux v0.2.3 Beta 5.
- Fixed engine sounds not updating during atmosphere-to-vacuum transitions.
- Fixed brief unmuffled audio after decoupling or undocking.

## 0.1a

- Initial release.
- Added vacuum muffling for active vessel audio.
- Added configurable muffling amount.
- Added smooth sound transition between atmosphere and vacuum.
- Added app bar button and Redux settings.
- Added English and Russian localization.
- Limited muffling amount to 25%, 50%, 75% and 100%; disabling the module restores stock sound behavior.
